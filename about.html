Welcome to Indiespoke! We are a collective of music lovers in Los Angeles, CA who want to shine a light on artist we think are incredible. Although "indie" is in our name, we want to hear from any genre. We want to focus on artist from anywhere and who come from anything. Our goal is to curate an eclectic body of work that will connect other music lovers with more artists.

For artist or bands who want to share their work, please send your information to indiespoke@gmail.com. We love hearing new stuff and don't be afraid, we don't bite. If we like your work we will make sure to post it and even write back to.
